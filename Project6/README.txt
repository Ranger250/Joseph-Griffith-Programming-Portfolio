-------------------------
# Title: Jumper
-------------------------

Platform jumper with boosts, enemies, and a highscore system.


-------------------------
# Installations
-------------------------

python 3.0 and later


-------------------------
# Use
-------------------------

Left and right arrow keys to move, space to jump. The longer you hold space down the higher you jump. 
There are also power ups that you can run into that give you a major boost upwards.
