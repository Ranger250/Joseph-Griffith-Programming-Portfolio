-------------------------
# Title: Shooty
-------------------------

A simple space shooter game. Asteroids come fromt the top, you shoot and avoid them.


-------------------------
# Installations
-------------------------

python 3.0 and later


-------------------------
# Use
-------------------------

Left and right arrow keys to move your ship, up arrow key makes the stars move(looks cool), spacebar shoots. 
You can pick up three kinds of power ups: more shields, better guns, and an extra life.
